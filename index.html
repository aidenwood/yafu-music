A combination of old and new.

I am on a defined mission of what must be accomplished. After speaking with a number of Native Shaman in the Peruvian mountains I knew what was already deep inside what to be my absolute purpose. I have always loved music from the age of soul and wondered to myself

from a young age, where did we go so wrong. When did we forget what was so perfect. Jazz and soul ages of music are a big inspiration to me, however I still try to pull in all the lessons we have learnt across all genres of music.
